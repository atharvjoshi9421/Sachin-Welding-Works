About us

Our Story

Sachin Welding Works, established in the year 2017, is a reputed manufacturer based in Pune, Maharashtra. The company specializes in producing a wide range of stainless steel products including Rectangular Stainless Steel Palki, Stainless Steel Hand Wash Kitchen Sink, 36 Inch Stainless Steel Barricade, and Stainless Steel Storage Tray Rack Trolley, along with various other custom-made items. With a strong commitment to quality and precision, the company has built a solid reputation in the market for delivering durable, functional, and aesthetically designed stainless steel solutions that cater to both industrial and domestic needs.

Under the capable leadership of Mr. Sachin Atmaram More, the firm operates as a proprietorship with a workforce of up to ten dedicated employees. The company conducts its operations from its registered address at First Floor, Flat No. 204, Prathmeshwara Apartment, Yewalwadi, Pune – 411046, Maharashtra, India. Its GST registration (27BMLPM4809F1ZV) was obtained on 24th January 2018.
